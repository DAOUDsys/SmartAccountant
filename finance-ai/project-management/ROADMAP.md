# Roadmap

This roadmap follows the product vision in `PRODUCT_SPEC.md`: Finance AI is an AI Accountant, and the chat experience is the primary product surface.

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Monorepo structure.
- Expo mobile app.
- NestJS backend.
- PostgreSQL and Prisma configuration.
- Shared TypeScript packages.
- Documentation and engineering conventions.

## Phase 2: Authentication and Chat Foundation

- Authentication.
- Chat UI.
- Conversation history.
- Conversation list.
- Message composer.
- Empty, loading, error, and retry states.

## Phase 3: AI Engine

- DeepSeek integration.
- Prompt builder.
- Tool calling.
- Conversation context.
- Structured outputs.
- AI response streaming.
- AI interpretation audit trail.

## Phase 4: Accounting Engine

- Transactions.
- Customers.
- Suppliers.
- Ledger.
- Payment allocation.
- Accounting validation rules.

## Phase 5: Inventory

- Products.
- Stock.
- Purchases.
- Sales.
- Inventory audit.
- Stock adjustments.

## Phase 6: Reports

- Customer statements.
- Inventory reports.
- Profit and loss.
- Expenses.
- Balance summary.
- AI-assisted report explanations.

## Phase 7: PDF Export

- Professional templates.
- Sharing.
- Printing.
- Export history.

## Phase 8: Dashboard

- Charts.
- Analytics.
- KPIs.
- Secondary business overview.

## Phase 9: Voice

- Speech to text.
- Voice commands.
- Audio recording.
- Voice permissions and recording states.

## Phase 10: Polish

- Offline support.
- Animations.
- Accessibility.
- Notifications.
- Performance hardening.
